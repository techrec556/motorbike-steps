Tenere 700 Mods

Explore my plans for upgrading the ultimate adventure bike

Security

Keeping the bike safe on and off the road.

Security mods include GPS trackers, alarms, and physical locks to keep my Tenere 700 protected wherever I go. I plan to integrate a tracking system that can alert me if my bike is moved without my consent. This will not only enhance security but also provide peace of mind when I'm away from my bike. Additionally, I’m considering installing a high-decibel alarm system that activates if someone tries to tamper with it, ensuring maximum protection.

Safety

Enhancing safety for all types of adventures.

Upgrading lights, adding crash bars, and enhancing braking systems are just some of the ways to make this bike as safe as possible. I’m looking to install LED lights that not only improve visibility during night rides but also consume less power. Additionally, crash bars will provide essential protection for the bike’s body and components in case of a fall, while a high-performance brake system will ensure that I can stop on a dime, especially in emergency situations.

Comfort

Enhancing the ride experience for long adventures.

Comfort upgrades such as improved seating, heated grips, and better wind protection will make long rides more enjoyable. I’m considering an aftermarket seat that offers better lumbar support and cushioning for those long hauls. Heated grips will ensure my hands stay warm during colder rides, and a taller windscreen will provide better wind deflection, reducing fatigue on longer trips. Overall, these upgrades aim to transform the Tenere 700 into a more comfortable adventure machine.

Practicality

Making the bike more versatile for daily use and adventures.

Practical mods will include adding luggage racks, upgrading the electrical system for accessories, and ensuring easy maintenance access. I’m looking to install a robust luggage system that can handle both everyday commuting and longer trips, ensuring I can carry all the gear I need. Additionally, enhancing the electrical system will allow for easy connectivity of devices, ensuring that I stay powered up on the road. With these practical upgrades, the Tenere 700 will be a reliable partner for any adventure.

Luxury

Elevating the riding experience with premium features.

Luxury upgrades might include custom upholstery, high-end audio systems, and advanced navigation setups. I’m considering a premium seat cover that adds both comfort and style, along with a Bluetooth-enabled sound system for an immersive audio experience on long rides. An upgraded navigation system will ensure I can find the best routes easily, adding a touch of convenience and sophistication to my adventures.
